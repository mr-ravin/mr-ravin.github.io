Think like an Engineer, act like a Businessman, feel like an Artist, and live like an Ocean.

Featuredravin

Everything is Science if you can understand it, and is an Art if you can feel it. He is an engineer by degree, a researcher by hobby, and an artist by nature.

A curious person blessed to see life as an artist, engineer, researcher, inventor, economist, poet, ethical hacker, mathematician, video game developer, and author. He is author of the book "The Engineer’s Plan", and runs a YouTube channel "CodeBeings" for sharing knowledge related to computer programming.

Some of his achievements are: a computer language, computer ransomware (and its antivirus), Addictive auction theory in Theoretical Economics, Adaptive Base Representation Theorem in Computing and Mathematics, Some theorems in Geometry and Pure Mathematics, Mathematical Developments in the Simulation Hypothesis in Physics, cutting edge novel research works in the field of Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, a novel algorithm in Quantum Computing, Cyclic scheduling algorithm in Operating Systems, Modified counting sort in Algorithms, and some Patented Inventions, with 50+ software projects and repositories on GitHub, and working experience in product-focused startups in the field of Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Natural Language Processing. Along with this, he has done some paintings and related work in the field of Arts, some poetry and is the author of the book “The Engineer’s Plan”. All these experiences have given him a broader view of life and help understand the world better.

Artist’s Life, spend most of his free time with art galleries, paintings, photography, and art related work. Learning to develop various art forms, from Digital art to sculptures, to paintings etc. For more details please do visit the Artist’s Life section.

Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering, during his graduation (2013-2017) he explored various fields of Computer Sciences to feed his ongoing curiosity. He got trained in Java SE and Java EE from NIIT during his first and second year of graduation and gained skills on Servlets, Java Server Pages, JDBC,  Java Beans, Java FX, and other interesting OOPS method. His first programming language was C++ and he is still pretty good in it. In the second year, he joined a workshop conducted by Lucideus for improving cybersecurity based skill set, which in long run helped him in developing a new Computer Virus written in Java SE 8 and later got copyrighted to him. In the third year, he got an internship in Big Data Hadoop from LinuxWorld, Rajasthan (tied with redhat) where he worked on Hadoop 1 and 2, Docker, Splunk, Hive, Pig, Python using RHEL OS and submitted his internship project “Smart Hadoop Cluster Tool” for the fulfilment of the internship. In the fourth year of graduation,  He and his team selected designing and developing a “Private Dock Cloud”  as their final year project for providing services such as SaaS, STaaS, IaaS, and OSaaS, using Docker build with Python in RHEL OS. All these experiences provided him massive exposure to cutting-edge technologies of that time. During his graduation, he was consistently working on his individual research works, papers and copyrights. Research works were got accepted and then published by Springer and other reputed publication houses. All the software-based projects except copyrighted software, are available on his github profile, present in Engineer’s Life section too.

He started his career in the field of Machine Learning, Deep Learning in 2017, and since then he is consistently working in the domain of Artificial Intelligence. He has also done various research works including novel research papers, copyrights and patents.

Sounds exciting !!! explore respective sections to know more, and also do checkout his YouTube Channel Code Beings.

He began his journey to explore ten different life paths in August 2013 and completed it on October 31, 2021. This page will no longer be updated, as that challenge was part of his 20s. The photo here is of Mr. Ravin Kumar, taken during his Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) studies in Computer Science (i.e., Engineering). He is grateful to his college self for envisioning such an adventurous challenge and having the courage to embark on this journey. He is now focused on pursuing something more meaningful.
